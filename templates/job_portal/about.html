SevenDyne: Your Trusted Remote Staffing & Training Partner

Since 2016, SevenDyne has been providing expert remote staffing solutions and professional training programs. As a leading consultancy, we specialize in connecting businesses with top talent and empowering professionals with the skills they need to succeed. From app development to HR payroll solutions, we continue to expand our global reach, serving clients across various industries and locations, including the UK, Germany, Malaysia, and the US.

Our Journey

2012: Began as a freelance agency, focusing on delivering quality staffing cum training solutions.
2016: Officially incorporated as SevenDyne, evolving into a full-service consultancy offering remote staffing and training services.
Since then, we have grown into a trusted partner for businesses looking for skilled professionals and high-quality training programs. We are proud to have onboarded over 10+ experienced professionals and completed a wide range of projects in diverse industries.
