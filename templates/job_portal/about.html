Sevendyne: Your Trusted Partner for IT Staffing & Professional Training

Since 2016, Sevendyne has been delivering premium IT staffing solutions and professional training services. Our journey began in 2012 as a freelance agency, and we incorporated as a company in 2016. We've successfully placed technology professionals and trained candidates for clients across the UK, Germany, Malaysia, US, and more, bringing deep expertise in diverse technology domains.

Technical Expertise

Our talent pool and training programs cover comprehensive technical domains including: - Full-stack Development (React, Angular, Django, Spring Boot) - Mobile App Development (Android, iOS) - C++ Qt Development - Java Enterprise Solutions - Solidworks Engineering - PLC SCADA Systems - Cloud & DevOps

Industry Experience

We've successfully delivered staffing and training solutions across diverse sectors: - Engineering Design & Manufacturing - Navigation & Mapping Systems - Property Management Solutions - Data Analytics & Business Intelligence - E-commerce & Digital Retail - Enterprise Software Solutions

Professional Staffing Services

Our staffing solutions provide businesses with skilled technology professionals, offering flexible engagement models with comprehensive payroll management. We handle the entire recruitment process, ensuring you get qualified professionals who match your technical requirements and company culture. Our transparent monthly pricing model and quality assurance processes ensure reliable, long-term staffing solutions.

Professional Training Programs

Our industry-focused training programs are designed for both students and working professionals. Our training programs are structured to build practical skills and industry-relevant expertise, helping participants advance their careers in technology.

Quality Assurance

At Sevendyne, we maintain rigorous quality standards in both our staffing and training services. Global Reach
