SevenDyne: Your Trusted Tech Staffing Partner

Since 2016, SevenDyne has been delivering end-to-end client support while connecting businesses with top talent. Our training programs empower professionals with the skills they need to succeed, and we help companies build high-performing teams for diverse roles. From project execution to staffing solutions, we continue to expand our global reach, serving clients in the UK, Germany, Malaysia, the US, and beyond.

Our Journey

2016: SevenDyne started as a tech talent-building initiative, assembling dedicated development teams for freelance clients. Over the years, we expanded our expertise, becoming a full-service provider of remote staffing and professional training.

Since our inception, we have successfully catered to more than 10 clients across the UK, Germany, the US, Malaysia, and India. Our focus is on sourcing high-quality professionals for technology-driven businesses while also nurturing future talent through structured training programs. We take pride in developing skilled interns, equipping them with industry-ready expertise, and assigning them to client projects, ensuring a seamless blend of capability and innovation.
