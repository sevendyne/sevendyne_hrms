SevenDyne offers expert mobile app development services with a decade of experience in delivering high-performance Android applications tailored to meet your specific needs. Based in Cochin, India, we specialize in creating robust, scalable apps that stand out in today’s competitive market.

Payment Gateway Project

Developed for Red Dot Payment Singapore clients, this Android app is designed to simulate and test payment acceptance workflows. The app allows users to send access tokens and process various payment scenarios, ensuring the secure and efficient handling of transactions. It serves as a critical tool for testing and validating payment gateway integrations before going live.

Pocket Parade Game Project

Created for RedRhino Games USA, Pocket Parade is an immersive mobile game app that draws inspiration from classic strategy games like Age of Empires. Players can create their own game worlds, build empires, and engage in strategic battles. The app was a trending hit, captivating users with its intricate gameplay, world-building features, and competitive warfare. It showcases Sevendyne’s ability to deliver high-quality gaming experiences on mobile platforms.

Ticket Generator Project

Developed for Gtechna, Montreal, Canada, this Android app is a demo app used by Canadian traffic enforcement agencies. The app features two main screens: Ticket Creation and Ticket Management, allowing officers to create and manage traffic violation records efficiently. Compatible with Android versions 2.2 to 4.2, it enhances law enforcement efficiency.
